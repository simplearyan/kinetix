# Faster, Smarter, packed with 50+ new designs in Kinetix

Today, we're excited to share a major update with you: Kinetix's visual generation is now **20% faster** and **18% more accurate**.

## Under the Hood

We've rewritten our layout engine from scratch using WebAssembly (Wasm). This allows for near-instant rendering of complex diagrams, even on mobile devices.

## 50+ New Designs

We've also expanded our library include:
-   **Sankey Diagrams**: Great for flow and volume.
-   **Org Charts**: improved hierarchy handling.
-   **Swimlanes**: For cross-functional process mapping.
