# Introducing Custom Generation

Get the visual you want, faster.

Today we're launching **Custom Generation** to give you more control over visuals - so you can get the visual you want, faster.

## What's New?

* **Style Control**: Choose between Corporate, Hand-Drawn, and Neon styles.
* **Layout Options**: Switch between Process flow and Cycle loops instantly.
* **Export**: Download as High-Res PNG or SVG.

Try it out in the app today!
