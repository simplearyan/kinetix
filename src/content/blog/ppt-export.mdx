# PPT Export & File Import - Now Live on Kinetix

We've got a big Kinetix update we think you'll love: You can now export visuals directly to PowerPoint, Google Slides, and more.

## Seamless Integration

Stop taking screenshots. With our new **Native Export**, your diagrams remain crisp vectors when you drop them into your presentation decks.

### Supported Formats

-   **PPTX**: Native PowerPoint slides.
-   **PDF**: High-quality vectors.
-   **SVG**: For web and design tools (Figma, Sketch).

## File Import

Got an existing outline? Drag and drop your `.txt` or `.md` files directly into Kinetix to instantly generate a visual structure.
